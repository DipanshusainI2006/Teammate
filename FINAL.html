About Teammate

Discover how Teammate helps students connect, collaborate, and bring ideas to life by matching the right skills with the right opportunities.

What is Teammate?

Teammate is a skill-matching platform built for students to easily find project or internship teammates. Whether you're just starting out or already experienced, Teammate connects you with like-minded peers to work on exciting real-world ideas.

Why use Teammate?

  • Match with teammates based on skills & interests
  • Explore and join innovative projects
  • Build your portfolio with collaborative work
  • Grow your professional network for the future
